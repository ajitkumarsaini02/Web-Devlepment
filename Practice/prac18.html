Meet Tom

A tomcat is an adult, typically unneutered, male cat. The term stems from the 1760 book The Life and Adventures of a Cat, which featured a character named Tom. Male cats are often more adventurous, bold, and territorial than females, while also being known for independence and purring.

Jerry dog content primarily features Spike the Bulldog, the iconic, muscular, and sometimes grumpy dog from the Tom & Jerry cartoons who protects Jerry mouse. He is known for his stern attitude towards Tom, his devotion to his son Tyke, and being a softie for mice.
